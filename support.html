Help Center

How can we help?

Find answers to common questions or reach out directly — we usually reply fast.

🗺️

Map & Live Status

The map shows real-time activity in your city. Status updates from other users appear within seconds. Make sure location is enabled for accurate results.

👤

Your Account

To change your username, display name, or profile photo, head to Settings in the app. To delete your account, go to Settings → Account → Delete Account.

🔔

Notifications

Control which alerts you receive in Settings → Notifications. If you're not getting push notifications, check your phone's notification permissions for Partied.

🔒

Privacy & Safety

You are fully in control of who can see your status. Adjust visibility in Settings → Privacy. You can also block or report any user from their profile.

🎪

Venues & Events

Venue owners can claim and manage their listing. If you're a venue interested in partnering with Partied, contact us using the link below.

🐛

Report a Bug

Found something broken? Email us with your device model, OS version, and a short description of what happened. Screenshots help a lot.

Still need help?

Our team is small but fast. Drop us an email and we'll get back to you as soon as possible — usually within a few hours.
